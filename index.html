Daily Health Tips स्वास्थ्य सुझाव

Stay Hydrated पानी पियें

Drink 8-10 glasses of clean water daily. Add lemon or mint for taste. Boil water if unsure for quality.

Daily Movement व्यायाम

30 minutes of walking, yoga or local sport. Even household work counts as physical activity.

Balanced Diet संतुलित आहार

Include fruits, vegetables, whole grains and proteins. Eat seasonal local produce for better nutrition.

Safe Sun Exposure धूप से बचाव

Avoid direct sun 10AM–4PM. Use hat, umbrella, etc. Some morning sun is beneficial.

Mental Wellness मानसिक स्वास्थ्य

Take breaks, practice deep breathing, and connect with loved ones for emotional well-being.

Regular Checkups नियमित जांच

Visit healthcare providers for preventive checkups even when feeling healthy.
